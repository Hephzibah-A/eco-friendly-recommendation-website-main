Alternative Eco-friendly Products

Plastic Pen


Plastic pens, ubiquitous in daily life, pose a significant environmental challenge. These single-use writing tools contribute to plastic pollution when discarded, taking centuries to decompose. Their production consumes fossil fuels and generates greenhouse gas emissions. Sustainable alternatives can help mitigate the environmental impact of plastic pens.


Impact on The Environment:


  • Materials and Production: Plastic
  • Use and Durability: Short-term usage & Not Durable
  • End-of-life impact: Doesn't Degrade easily, Affects the Environment
